About StudioWave Engine

Multi-track WebClient DAW

Welcome to StudioWave, a fully functional, high-performance, 100% web-based multi-track audio workstation. Designed around modern Angular 21, the application requires zero external servers and performs all DSP, decoding, and compiling tasks entirely inside your client browser.

🎥 Video Extraction

By processing binary file streams using local FileReader and AudioContext decoders, StudioWave isolates and extracts the audio streams from uploaded videos directly inside the browser sandbox.

🎛️ Pitch & Speed Processing

Uses high-performance resampling and buffer rate scaling to translate semitones into relative playback Speeds. Changes are Hot-swapped on playing nodes for instant tactile control.

◀️ Instant Reversal

Clones channel arrays and reverses floating-point vectors on the fly, creating cached reverse buffers. Flipping the reverse switch hot-swaps playing streams seamlessly.

📀 Offline rendering mix

Uses CPU-optimized OfflineAudioContexts to render all track gains, panning, offsets, and pitches down to a CD-quality 44.1kHz buffer, compiling standard 16-bit PCM WAV containers.
